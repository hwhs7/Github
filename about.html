About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of students passionate about spreading awareness of first aid and emergency readiness. Our goal is to make first aid knowledge simple, clear, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should know how to respond in a crisis. With proper training and resources, you can be the difference between life and death. This site is a small step toward that goal.
